What Are Dental Implants?

Dental implants are artificial roots placed into your jaw to support replacement teeth or bridges. They're a long-lasting and natural-looking solution for missing teeth. At Noble Dental, we use the most advanced techniques and biocompatible materials to ensure safety and durability.

Benefits of Dental Implants:

  • Improved Appearance and Confidence
  • Permanent and Durable Tooth Replacement
  • Enhanced Oral Health and Functionality
  • No Damage to Adjacent Teeth
  • Prevents Bone Loss

Frequently Asked Questions

Are dental implants painful?

No. The procedure is performed under local anesthesia and is virtually painless. Post-procedure discomfort is minimal and manageable.

How long do implants last?

With proper care and maintenance, dental implants can last 15 years or more.

Is everyone suitable for dental implants?

Most healthy adults are. A consultation with our implantologist will determine your eligibility.
