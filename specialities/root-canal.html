Tooth Saving

Root Canal Treatment (RCT) in Nallagandla

Painless, precise, single-visit RCT with RVG & apex locator.

When do you need an RCT?

  • Severe lingering pain to hot/cold
  • Night pain, swelling, or abscess
  • Deep decay visible on X-ray

How we make it comfortable

  • Computer-guided anesthesia
  • Rubber dam isolation
  • Rotary files + apex locator

RCT FAQs

Is it safe during pregnancy?

Yes—timed with your trimester and proper shielding.

Cost & time?

From ₹3,500; 45–90 minutes for simple cases.

Will my tooth feel normal?

Yes, after initial healing; a crown improves strength and comfort.
