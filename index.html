Common Dental Problems — What It Means & What To Do

Short, plain-English guidance for symptoms people search. Tap a card to learn the next step or jump to the right treatment.

Toothache / Sensitivity

Sharp or lingering pain with hot/cold may be decay, cracked tooth, or exposed dentin.

  • Do now: Warm salt-water rinse; avoid very hot/cold; OTC pain relief if safe.
  • Likely care: Filling, Root Canal, fluoride/desensitizer, or crown.
Fillings Book Call

Swelling / Dental Abscess

Local swelling, throbbing pain, pus taste, fever = likely infection at tooth or gum.

  • Do now: Cold compress outside cheek; do not apply heat; seek urgent care.
  • Likely care: Drainage + antibiotics (if indicated) and RCT or extraction.
Pain Relief & RCT Urgent Visit

Broken / Cracked Tooth

Fracture from biting hard foods, trauma, or old fillings; pain on biting common.

  • Do now: Save any piece; avoid chewing on that side.
  • Likely care: Bonding or crown; deep cracks may need RCT.
Crowns Bonding

Knocked-Out Tooth (Avulsion)

Permanent tooth out of socket. Time-critical (best within 30–60 minutes).

  • Do now: Handle by the crown, rinse gently, reinsert if possible or store in milk/saliva.
  • Likely care: Reimplantation + splint; follow-up for RCT if needed.
Emergency Call Come In Now

Wisdom Tooth Pain / Pericoronitis

Inflamed gum flap over a partially erupted molar; pain, bad taste, swelling.

  • Do now: Warm salt-water rinses; gentle cleaning; avoid hard foods.
  • Likely care: Cleaning + antibiotics (if indicated) or extraction.
Wisdom Extraction Book

Bleeding Gums / Gingivitis

Plaque-related inflammation; bleeding on brushing/flossing, bad breath.

  • Do now: Don’t stop brushing; add floss/interdental brush; use soft brush.
  • Likely care: Scaling & polishing; gum therapy if pockets present.
Scaling Gum Treatment

Loose Tooth (Adult)

Often due to gum disease or trauma; mobility may be reversible early on.

  • Do now: Avoid wiggling/biting hard foods; keep area clean.
  • Likely care: Deep cleaning, splinting, bite adjustment; advanced cases need grafts/extraction + implant.
Periodontal Care Implant Options

Bad Breath (Halitosis)

Commonly from tongue coating, gum disease, dry mouth, or cavities.

  • Do now: Brush tongue; hydrate; avoid tobacco; consider sugar-free gum.
  • Likely care: Treat gum disease/cavities; saliva management; review diet/meds.
Cleaning Fix Cavities

Mouth Ulcers / Canker Sores

Small painful sores from stress, trauma, diet, or deficiencies.

  • Do now: Avoid spicy/acidic foods; topical soothing gels; B-complex if advised.
  • Likely care: Identify triggers; medicated gels; rule out systemic causes for recurrent ulcers.
Get Checked

Teeth Grinding (Bruxism)

Jaw clenching at night; wear facets, jaw pain, headaches.

  • Do now: Heat on muscles; gentle jaw stretches; reduce caffeine late.
  • Likely care: Night guard; bite balancing; stress/sleep counseling.
Night Guard

Jaw Pain / Clicking (TMJ)

Joint/muscle disorder; clicking, limited opening, morning stiffness.

  • Do now: Soft diet; avoid wide yawns; warm compress.
  • Likely care: Splints, physio, habit therapy; imaging if persistent.
Assessment

Tooth Decay / Cavity

Brown/black spots, food lodgment, sensitivity.

  • Do now: Reduce sugar frequency; floss; fluoride toothpaste twice daily.
  • Likely care: Filling; deep cases need RCT + crown.
Fillings

Stained / Yellow Teeth

Surface stains (tea/coffee/tobacco) or internal discoloration.

  • Do now: Limit staining foods; rinse after; non-abrasive paste.
  • Likely care: Scaling & polishing, whitening, veneers for intrinsic stains.
Whitening Veneers

Generalized Sensitivity

Worn enamel/recession; zingers to cold/sweets.

  • Do now: Desensitizing toothpaste; soft brush; avoid aggressive brushing.
  • Likely care: Fluoride/desensitizers, treat gum recession, fix high bites.
Checkup

Crooked / Spaced Teeth

Crowding, gaps, bite problems affect function and smile.

  • Do now: Early evaluation age 7+; maintain flossing.
  • Likely care: Braces or Clear Aligners; retainers post-treatment.
Braces Clear Aligners

Kids’ Tooth Pain / Cavities

Baby teeth get cavities fast; affects eating/sleep and adult tooth health.

  • Do now: Brush with pea-size fluoride paste; limit sticky snacks/juices.
  • Likely care: Fillings, pulpotomy/pulpectomy, stainless steel crowns; sealants for prevention.
Pediatric Care

Missing Tooth / Space

Gaps shift bite and stress other teeth over time.

  • Do now: Keep area clean; consider temporary flipper if visible.
  • Likely care: Implant, bridge, or denture based on case/bone.
Implants Bridges Dentures

Gum Recession / Notching

Exposed roots cause sensitivity and aesthetics concerns.

  • Do now: Softer brush; correct technique; desensitizing paste.
  • Likely care: Bite adjustment, bonding, grafts for advanced cases.
Grafting

Denture Sore Spots / Loose Denture

Pressure points or poor fit; affects chewing and speech.

  • Do now: Remove overnight; rinse mouth; avoid self-adjustment.
  • Likely care: Relining, repairs, or implant-supported dentures for stability.
Denture Care Implant Denture

After Tooth Removal / Dry Socket

Pain peaking day 2–3 can indicate dry socket.

  • Do now: Bite on gauze 30–45 min; no spitting/straws/smoking; soft diet.
  • Likely care: Dressing/medicated packing; pain control; review hygiene.
Extraction Care

Braces Wire Poking / Bracket Off

Irritation from wires or detached bracket between visits.

  • Do now: Orthodontic wax on the wire; sterile nail clipper to trim only if necessary.
  • Likely care: Quick in-clinic fix; keep next review.
Ortho Review

Pregnancy Dental Care

Gums may bleed more; safe dental care is possible with precautions.

  • Do now: Soft brush + floss; small frequent meals; rinse after nausea.
  • Likely care: Cleanings, fillings, emergency care; coordinate meds/x-rays as needed.
Pregnancy-Safe Care

White/Red Patches (Oral Lesions)

Persistent patches or non-healing sores need evaluation.

  • Do now: Avoid tobacco/alcohol; document duration.
  • Likely care: Exam, possible biopsy, targeted treatment or referral.
Oral Screening

Kids’ Mouth Ulcers / Teething

Teething causes irritability; ulcers from bites/viral causes.

  • Do now: Cool teething rings; gentle gels as advised; fluids.
  • Likely care: Counseling, topical care; rule out dehydration/fever management.
Pediatric Visit

Gummy Smile / Short Teeth

Excess gum display or worn teeth affects smile line.

  • Do now: Photo documentation; evaluate grinding habits.
  • Likely care: Gum contouring, bonding/veneers, ortho or aligners as needed.
Smile Design

Implant Tenderness / Aftercare

Normal mild soreness early; persistent pain/swelling needs review.

  • Do now: Soft diet; chlorhexidine rinse as directed; avoid smoking.
  • Likely care: Bite adjustments, hygiene review, treat peri-implantitis if present.
Implant Review

Post-Whitening Sensitivity

Transient zings common after bleaching.

  • Do now: Avoid extremes 48 hrs; use desensitizing gel/paste.
  • Likely care: Fluoride varnish; adjust concentration/exposure time next session.
Whitening Care

Lost / Broken Retainer

Teeth may shift without retention after braces/aligners.

  • Do now: Wear backup if available; contact clinic quickly.
  • Likely care: New retainer or minor tooth alignment touch-ups.
Retainers

Kids’ Chipped / Displaced Tooth

Common with falls; primary vs permanent needs different care.

  • Do now: Control bleeding; cold compress; store fragment.
  • Likely care: Smoothening/bonding; monitor nerve; splint if displaced.
Pediatric Trauma Care

Gap Between Teeth (Diastema)

Spaces from size mismatch or habits; cosmetic + functional impact.

  • Do now: Break thumb-sucking/tongue thrust habits; evaluation needed.
  • Likely care: Aligners/braces, bonding, or veneers; retainers to hold.
Close Gaps Veneers

Sports Mouth Guard

Protects teeth/jaw during contact sports.

  • Do now: Use a temporary boil-and-bite if playing soon.
  • Likely care: Custom guard for comfort and protection; replace yearly.
Custom Guard
