Types of Braces We Offer

  • Metal Braces: reliable, budget‑friendly, precise control.
  • Ceramic Braces: tooth‑coloured brackets for a discreet look.
  • Self‑Ligating Braces: lower friction; fewer chairside visits.

Treatment Journey

  1. Consultation, exam and digital scan (or impressions).
  2. Customized plan with estimated duration and review schedule.
  3. Bracket bonding + archwire placement; periodic adjustments.
  4. Retention phase with retainers to maintain alignment.

Who is a Good Candidate?

Crowding, gaps, deep bite, crossbite, open bite or protrusion. We assess growth stage, oral hygiene and lifestyle to select the best option.

Care Tips

  • Brush after meals; use interdental brushes and fluoridated toothpaste.
  • Avoid hard/sticky foods that may dislodge brackets.
  • Wear elastics/retainers exactly as advised.

FAQs

How long does braces treatment take?

Typical duration is 12–18 months depending on age, bite complexity and compliance with elastics/appointments.

Do braces hurt?

You may feel pressure or soreness for 2–3 days after adjustments. OTC pain relief and soft foods help during this period.

Will I need retainers?

Yes. Retainers maintain alignment after active treatment. Skipping retainers can allow teeth to drift.
